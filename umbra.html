Umbra Press Kit


Story

You are Umbra, one of the few surviving members of your kind. You find yourself in the mysterious depths of treacherous caverns trying to find a way for your people to carry on, but you aren’t alone…

There’s another race of creatures taking the opportunity to claim the caverns for themselves. They are quick and violent, and difficult to deal with alone. But you are all that’s left to protect your people and the place you call home.

Gameplay

As Umbra you will be ascending the dark caverns to find the source of these creatures of light. As you get closer and closer to their home they get stronger and stronger. You will see ruins and remains of fighting, and along the way you may even discover something… mysterious.

Hidden Secrets

Those with a knack for exploration will have plenty to find in the back corners and hidden walls of the caverns. You’ll just have to wait and see what you can find…

Boss Fights

There will be challenging encounters littered throughout the caverns. They will test your skills, prowess, wit and determination in a plethora of ways, requiring you to think carefully about the mechanics and powers in your possession.

Animation Synopsis

From The Perspective of Umbra

‘They’re all gone’ I say in my head. ‘There’s hardly any of us left and I’m the only one fit enough to get food. I can’t believe this happened… I don’t know how this happened… it was so fast.’ I look down at my family, or rather what’s left of my family.

‘I need to go looking again. There’s nothing to eat nearby, but I might be able to look up higher in the caverns.’ I know very well that its dangerous, but the current lack of food is more so, especially with the state some of us are in. I walk past the homes of the Tenebri who used to live here, but they’re long since emptied. The magnitude of skulls littered around the town is gut wrenching. I’d love to clean them up and decorate them respectfully, but right now we need food.

I continue ascending into the caverns until I reach the border, the cliff no Tenebri have crossed for centuries, but I must. I'm feeling colder and colder as I carry on. Something feels off. ‘No... it can't be...’ I don't my eyes. ‘A papyroot? I can gather seeds while I’m here and re-sow our farms!’ But the moment of happiness doesn’t last. I see weird glowing creatures off in the distance, and they’re approaching slowly closer.

I run. I don’t dare to look back as I know they’ll be chasing me. I thought the Lucidus were a myth, and even if they weren’t they shouldn't be this close for another 20 years! I see one lunge at me, missing to the side.

‘I think I’m losing them.’ But I can’t slow down now. ‘Oh no...’ They’re in front of me. I turn around but there are more there. I'm trapped.

FAQ

May I use the images on this website?

You may use any image from this website so long as you do not make any changes to the image itself and you link back to this website. You also may not sell the images on this site.

May I create online content of me playing this game? (On YouTube, Twitch etc.)

You may stream or upload content of this game, although you will need to disable music in the settings menu. Also make sure to link the purchase sites for the game in a clear way.

Why aren't I allowed to use the game's soundtrack?

If you wish to upload the soundtrack or contain it in gameplay footage you will need to contact Christopher Larkin at:

composerlarkin@gmail.com

Can I create merchandise?

Its really cool if you make merchandise! But we ask that you don’t sell it or advertise it online. This is so people are not confused as to whether or not it was made by Corn Dog Games. If you would like to make official merch contact us at:

corndoggames@mail.com

How was Umbra developed?

Umbra was developed using the Unity game engine and programmed in C#. All art assets were created in Adobe Photoshop and animated in Adobe Animate.
